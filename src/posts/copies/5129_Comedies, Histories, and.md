The earliest known owner of this copy was Prince Augustus Frederick, Duke of Sussex (1773-1843), whose eldest brother was King George IV. Prince Augustus followed in his brother’s footsteps of a complicated love life, causing his parents endless trouble with hasty marriages. He also followed his brother’s example by purchasing a copy of the First Folio. George had purchased his Folio while he was still the Prince of Wales c 1800, while Prince Augustus acquired his copy c 1820.
