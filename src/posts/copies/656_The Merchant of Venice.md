Robert Harding Evans (1778-1857), sold 6 June 1815 for £3 15s to Richard Heber (1773-1833). Heber sold 5 June 1834, for £12 to Thomas Thorpe (fl. 1830). George Vernon (1803-1866), sold c. 1840 to Robert Stayne Holford (1808-1892) and son. Holford, sold 1925 to A.S.W. Rosenbach. Purchased from Rosenbach in 1951-52.

Zachary Lesser, Ghosts, Holes, Rips and Scrapes: Shakespeare in 1619, Bibliography in the Longue Durée (University of Pennsylvania Press, 2021), 18.
