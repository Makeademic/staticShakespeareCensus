The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

For Folger copy 17, wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also πA1,3,4.  Leaves πA1 and πA2 (title page) are supplied in facsim.  Leaf πA6 is supplied from another copy.  Also supplied from another copy are *1-4 and A1-2. Wants port. (on title page). Also wants all of Cymb., 3b1-3d4 (including the colophon). Supplied from another copy are leaves B3.4 (likely supplied), K1-5, N3.4, and 2y1-3a6. Imperfections affect text on l4, slightly on A5 and N3, and very slightly on H6, I1, X2, f4, and x6.
