The copies listed here cannot be identified with either issue of Love's Martyr.

Because this copy lacks all leaves before C2 and after Y4, it is impossible to tell to which issue of the edition these sheets originally belonged. Also lacking the lower half of leaves C2-4 and H1. All remaining leaves restored and extended c. 1850 from what was once nearly a circular residue of text.  A typescript note is included, describing this as "the fourth known copy" of Loves martyr.
