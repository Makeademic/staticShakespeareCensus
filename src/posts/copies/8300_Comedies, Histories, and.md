The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1685 folio edition.

Folger copy 30 wants the "verses" leaf with port. ([pi]1), the title leaf ([pi]2), and all else before C6 (other prelims., Temp., and Two Gent.).  The rest of the Comedies (Merry Wives through W.T.) are misbound at end. Also wanting are G6 (in Errors) and 4A6-end (all of Locrine but the first page). A narrow copy: fore-edge closely trimmed, affecting some rules at the  outer margin and adjacent text somewhat on a few pages. Imperfections affect text slightly on H2, *3E6 verso, and 3H6 and very slightly on X2 recto, 2R6 verso, 3C1 verso, 3G3, 3S4, 3Z1 recto, and 4A5.
