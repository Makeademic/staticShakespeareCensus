Tipped-in on rear flyleaf is the biography of Shakespeare from Fuller's Worthies (1662). On rear pastedown is summary by the Cognards of ancestry of Capel's wife, Elizabeth Percy, and her descent from Henry Percy, 9th Earl of Northumberland, and on provenance of the volume. The volume likely remained at Cassiobury, the Capel manor, until its sale in 1922.
