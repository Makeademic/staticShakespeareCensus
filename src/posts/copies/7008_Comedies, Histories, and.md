This issue is dated 1663, with verses "To the Reader" on A1v and the title page on [A2]r. Some copies include the portrait on the title page and some do not. Only copies with none of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

No title leaf, no "verses" leaf, and no additional plays present. Supplied are facsimiles of the 1663 "verses" leaf (-πA1) and the 1663 title leaf (-πA2). Wants πA3; supplied in facsimile signed "F.S by H junr. [i.e., by John Harris, Jr.]". Leaf πb1 is remargined at the tail.  Leaf πb6 is remargined at the head. Imperfections affect text very slightly on I4, Y5, 2A6, 2K5, 3D5, and 3M2,6. Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
