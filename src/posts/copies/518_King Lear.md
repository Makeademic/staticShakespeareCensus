Binding: not contemporary with text. Bound in full dark brown crushed levant morocco. Covers have double fillet borders and azure corner ornaments tooled in gilt. The spine is in six panels, divided by raised bands. Title gilt stamped in the second, and "London, 1608" in the third compartment. The inside of the covers feature full leather doublures of maroon crushed levant morocco with gilt tooled bold arabesque pattern of convolute fillet sprays with azured leaf ornaments and with a central open cartouche of inlaid dark olive morocco. Gilt over marbled edges, by David. Housed in a dark-green morocco case.
