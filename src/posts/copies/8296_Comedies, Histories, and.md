The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1685 folio edition.

Folger copy 12 wants the title leaf ([pi]2).  The "verses" leaf with port. ([pi]1), cut down and mounted, is mutilated, affecting the right edge of the port. and the last 2 lines. Port. in state 4. Leaf [superscript pi]A1 is mounted, with a window opened in the mount revealing the text on the verso. Outer corners of [superscript pi]A1 through A2 are mutilated, affecting text on [superscript pi]A1 recto, A1 verso, and A2 verso, and some rules. The outer corners and margins of 4A3,4, 4B5,6, and 4C1 are mutilated and reinforced with strips, affecting text on 4C1 and slightly on 4A3.  Leaf 4C2 is cut down to the rules on the recto and mounted. Closely trimmed at the head, affecting rules recto and verso, are: B5, D3, D4, E3, F6, and G6. Leaf *3D3 is mutilated in the outer margin, affecting rules, and *3D6 is closely trimmed at the tail, affecting rule, recto. Other imperfections affect text on 2G1, 2O5, slightly on 2H5,6, 2I4,5, 2R1 verso, 2U2, 3F6, 3K1, and 4B5, and very slightly on B2, H5, 2N2, *3C2, and 3K5 recto.
