This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at the signe of the Blacke Beare" in "Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

In Folger copy 35, πA1, 2 (title leaf), 5, and 6 are patched, affecting the inner or lower margins and slightly the port. on πA2 recto (title page).  Leaf πA6 is misbound before π5.  Leaf 3d4 (inlaid and completed in manuscript facsim.), with a watermark that redounds that in 3d1, is supplied from another copy. Port. (on title page) in state 3. The colophon appears on 3d4 recto as expected and also on 3d2 recto overprinting text.  See James G. McManaway,"The colophon of the second folio of Shakespeare," Library, 5th Ser., v. 9, p. 199-200. Imperfections affect text slightly on C1, 2, F1, 4, O6, x2, 2o5, and 2r6, and very slightly on 2A6, 2B2, b5, n5, 2a6, and 2b6.
