*1-4 & A1 from another, shorter copy, “To The Reader” leaf in facsimile, title-page tipped-in. Leaf kk3 also appears to be from another copy: it is shorter than others, has no watermark and neither does its conjugate kk4 (ZL). Final leaf with colophon also appears to be from another copy. A wormhole begins at top inner margin on A2 and ends on A6, possibly indicating that A1 is from a different copy (ZL). Burn holes scattered throughout the Comedies and one in King John, not elsewhere. Imprint on Lear p. 329 of something (glasses? scissors?).
