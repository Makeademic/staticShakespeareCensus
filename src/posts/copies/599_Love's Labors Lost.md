Text block has split at the t.p. of Hamlet (item 3 in binding) but appears sound otherwise. All quartos bound in this volume have been entirely disbound and the leaves inlaid into paper mounts measuring 233 x 183 mm. The first free endpaper is detaching; tear at foot of leaf E1, and some minor discolouration due to water damage. This volume contains: 1. Loves Labours Lost, 1631 (STC 22295); 2. Romeo and Juliet, 1637 (STC, 22326); 3. Hamlet, 1637 (STC, 22279); 4. The Merry wives of Windsor, 1630 (STC, 22301); 5. Henry the Fourth, pt. 1, 1639 (STC, 22287); 6. Othello, 1630 (STC, 22306). This is volume 2 of Malone's collection.
