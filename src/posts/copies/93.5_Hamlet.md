59

ROBINSON. 7 x 4 7/8". Title worn and soiled; 2 leaves mended in margins.\r\n"George Steevens" and "W.E.Burton" on title. Woodcut of arms of Charles I\r\nof England pasted on verso title. The Steevens (May, 1800, No. 1264), Burton\r\n(October, 1860, No. 4659), Phinney (bequeathed to his daughter, Mrs. Wirt\r\nRobinson) copy. Bound in yellow morocco.

It is unclear who pasted the woodcut arms of Charles I into this book, but it is unlikely to have been the King himself. After passing from Alice Phinney Robinson to her husband, Wirt Robinson, upon her death, the copy then passed to his second wife, Nancy Hinman Robinson, upon his death. Acquired by Blackwell from Nancy Hinman Robinson in the early 1940s, and passed down in her family until 2000 when it was acquired by Baran and Federighi. Then auctioned at Sotheby's, 28 June 2024, $144,000, buyer unknown.
