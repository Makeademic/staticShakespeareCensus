The copy at the Bodleian Library, which is reproduced in this volume, measures 7 3/16 x 5 7/16". It is inlaid, and forms part of a volume of seven Shakespeare quartos which were bound together by Malone and labelled 'Shakespeare Old Quartos, Vol. III.' The volume, which is numbered Malone 34, opens with Lucrece, 1594; and is followed by the Sonnets, 1609 (Aspley imprint); by Hamlet, 1607; by Love's Labour's Lost, 1598; by this edition of Pericles, 1609; by the 1619 edition of Pericles; and by A Yorkshire Tragedy, 1608.
