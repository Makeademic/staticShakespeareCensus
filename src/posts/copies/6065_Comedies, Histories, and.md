Various MS ownership inscriptions. To the blank recto of the 'To the reader' leaf: : "William Watts his book 1693"; "Samuel Sampson 1673"; Anne Browne (c. 1700); Richard Waddon (18th century?); Matthias Dampfer, with a bewigged portrait (18th century?); Charles Sampson; and the surname 'Witherwoods', 18th century. Title page inscribed 'E libris N. Dalton, E: Col: Reg. Oxon' in a hand of the early nineteenth century (this is Nathaniel Dalton, of Cucklington, Somerset, matric. Queen's Oxford 1772 aged 18). To the blank verso of the final leaf are several of the names seen on the blank recto of the 'To the reader leaf', with the addition of 'Thomas Napper', of the seventeenth century.  Pencilled price £48 to front endpaper, possibly Sandars' price. Rear endpaper with pencilled notes (by booksellers?) on the size of this copy, and prices of others sold. One begins 'Lilly June 1855' and ends 'coll[ated] & perf[ect] CDG'.
