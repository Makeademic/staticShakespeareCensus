Note on verso of title page by Malone: "It is observable that the first sheet of this play is marked with the letter R. The reason is, that it was printed at the same time with the Whole Contention of the Two Houses of Yorke and Lancaster, which ends with the Letter Q. That Copy of the two parts of K,. Henry VI. printed by T.P. (i.e. Thomas Pavier) which has no dates, is by this means ascertained to have been published in 1619.”
