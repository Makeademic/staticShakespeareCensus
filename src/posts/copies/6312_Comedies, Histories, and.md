This issue lists Richard Hawkins as the publisher.

This copy is associated with Thomas Hanmer's edition (Oxford, 1770–71). Both the "verses" leaf and the title leaf are damaged, trimmed and inlaid. Part of Jonson's poem is supplied in MS. Imprint and parts of the engraved portrait also supplied in MS. Last page of Cymbeline is missing and supplied in MS. Preface to the 2nd edition of Hanmer's Shakespeare bound in at end, with MS note indicating it was written by Hawkins.
