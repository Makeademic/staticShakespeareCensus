William A. White copy, with his signature and "June 7, 1906" on front flyleaf. On front flyleaf, White has recorded that the copy was bought at Sotheby's by Quaritch for £125, and that "BQ says 'a fine copy'. This came out of a vol. cont'g M of V. Oldcastle MND &c. Edw. Hussey sale."  Pasted onto a rear flyleaf is another note by White: "A fine copy of the second edition. The first was published in 1608, of which I have a photostat of the Huntington Copy. My copy came from the Edward Hussey Sale May 26, 1906. At the same sale there were the other eight spuriously dated quartos, showing that evidently an original volume had been broken up. They were all fine copies. The measurements given in the catalogue were not entirely accurate. This copy measures 7 1/16 by 5 1/4 inches."

Zachary Lesser, Ghosts, Holes, Rips and Scrapes: Shakespeare in 1619, Bibliography in the Longue Durée (University of Pennsylvania Press, 2021), 74, 156n15.
