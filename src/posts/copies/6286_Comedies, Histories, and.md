The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Folger copy 57 is nominally of the first issue, as formulated by William B. Todd, "Issues and states of the Second Folio and Milton’s epitaph on Shakespeare," Studies in bibliography, v. 5 (1952-53), p. 81-108.  However, the issue cannot be definitively determined.  This copy wants πA2 (title leaf) but contains πA5 ("Effigies" leaf) proper to the first issue (in state Ib). Wants port. (on title page). Also wants πA1,3. Leaves πA4,5,6 are misbound in reverse order. Also wants *1,2. Leaves *3,4 are misbound in reverse order. Leaf *3 is mutilated at the head, affecting the headpiece. Wants all but a stub of G2.  Wants M1.  Wants 3c3-end (including the colophon). Imperfections affect text slightly on d6 and very slightly on V3, d5, e6, f1, i4, and l2,3.
