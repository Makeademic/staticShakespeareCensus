This issue lists H. Herringman, E. Brewster, and R. Bentley as publishers in the imprint.

In Folger copy 11, the "verses" leaf with port. ([pi]1) has the margins excised and is inlaid and mounted. Port. in state 4 (mutilated at the top). Title leaf ([pi]2) is patched, with no effect on text. Imperfections affect text on 2B1 and 2G3 recto and very slightly on F4 verso, N2 verso, Y2, 2M4, 2U2, 3F1, 3R5, 3T2, 3Z3, and 4A1. Report of a detailed examination of this copy by G.E. Dawson in 1960 on file.
