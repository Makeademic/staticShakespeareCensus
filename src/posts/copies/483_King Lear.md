Autograph of George Lindsay Holford. Purchased from Martin Bodmer by H.P. Kraus, who offered it for sale in 1974 for $150,000, with description: "The present copy is entirely complete; none of the few marginal mends have affected the printing. It is a very large copy, its height of 185 mm. being exceeded only by the 190 mm. of the Huntington copy, and its breadth of 132 being exceeded only by the 137 mm. of the Harvard copy. All other copies are smaller in both dimensions. The measurements of the present copy, as given in the Bartlett-Pollard Census, are incorrect." In his autobiography, Kraus reports selling it to Yale for $140,000.
