This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be sold at his shop at the signe of the blacke Beare in Pauls Church-yard"; the original sheet πA2.5 was replaced by a cancel, which is printed on thicker paper and probably dates from 1641, after Thomas Cotes died. [DEEP]

Folger copy 42 is nominally of STC 22274e.3  However, the issue cannot be definitively determined.  This copy contains πA(πA2) (cancellans title leaf) proper to the second issue, but in place of its conjugate, πA(πA5) (cancellans "Effigies" leaf) proper to the second issue, this copy contains πA5 (cancellandum "Effigies" leaf) proper to the first issue (in state Ib); either (or both) of these leaves could be supplied from another copy.  The title leaf present is cut down, lacking the last line of the imprint, and is mounted, and the "Effigies" leaf present is extended. Wants πA1,3,4.  Present as prelim. leaves are, in the order bound: facsim. of 1st Folio leaf πA5; πA(πA2) (cancellans title leaf proper to the second issue; condition as recorded above); facsim. of 1st Folio πA6; πA5 (cancellandum "Effigies" leaf proper to the first issue; condition as recorded above); πA6; facsim. of 1st Folio πA5+2; *1 (mutilated--the headpiece excised--and patched); and *2-4. Port. (on title page) in state 3. Elsewhere this copy wants leaves or contains leaves demonstrably supplied from other copies or contains leaves questionable by reason of their idiosyncratic size or marks of soil: H3, genuine; N1.6, perhaps supplied; R6, likely supplied; 2B4, likely supplied; 2B5, wanting (supplied is the leaf bearing the corresponding text in the 3rd Folio--2B5); b1-5, perhaps supplied; d1,4,6, probably genuine; u1-4, perhaps supplied; u5-y6 (Hen. VIII entire), wanting (supplied are the leaves bearing some of the corresponding text in the 1st Folio--t6, v1,6, and x1-4, with v6 and x1-3 mutilated, affecting text); 2h2-5, perhaps supplied; and 2q3.4, perhaps supplied.  Of Cymb.: 3b6, supplied; 3c1, wanting; 3c2-5, perhaps supplied; 3c6, supplied; and 3d1-4 (including the colophon), wanting. Imperfections affect text on T2, slightly on F3, r1, and 2b4, and very slightly on I3, Q1, b4, p1, 2m1, and 2t4.
