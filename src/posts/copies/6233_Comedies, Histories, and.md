Signature of Henry Foss. In small lettering underneath imprint, "G. De C. C.". Pasted-in ms. note records the circumstances of acquisition (written by Foss?): "This copy of the Second Edition of Shakespeare's Plays was purchased at the Sale of Duplicates from the British Museum in February 1819 made in consequence of the purchase of the Library of the Revd Dr. Burney. Dr. Burney obtained this book in the following manner--At George Steevens's Sale there was a copy of this Edition which had the autograph of Charles 1st. Mr. Nichol bid for it for the King and Dr. Burney was his opponenent but the Dr. yeilded [sic] when he understood for whom Mr. Nichol wished to purchase it: the king being informed of the circumstance was so pleased with Dr. B's conduct that he desired this copy from his own library might be sent to Dr B. with his thanks for his giving up King Charles's copy."
