This issue differs in the setting of the title page from the other two, and it lists H. Herringman as the publisher in the imprint and Joseph Knight and Francis Saunders as the booksellers.

An extra sheet (Y3.4) is bound-in. The running title on the second page of "The two gentlemen of Verona" (p. 19) is erroneously printed as "The tempest." It appears that sheets 2G1.6 and 2G2.5 were each inserted from different copies, as the conjugate pairs share matching worm holes found neither in adjacent gatherings, nor in the other sheets that comprise 2G.
