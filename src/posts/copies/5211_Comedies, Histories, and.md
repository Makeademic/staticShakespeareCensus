In none of the states is Troilus included in the "Catalogue" of plays.

It is somewhat unusual that this copy contains two variant that exist in the second state of three possible states. Due to the nature of the printing process of the Folio there are few leaves that even have a third state, and then to find a leaf in the second of those three states is fairly rare...A somewhat interesting manuscript notation found in this copy is the autograph of George Duckett (1684-1732). Duckett was a politician and author who traded regular barbs with Alexander Pope. He is indirectly attacked by Pope in his Dunciad and one of Duckett’s many attacks on Pope was Pope Alexander's Supremacy and Infallibility Examin'd, written with John Dennis.
