The copy in the Bodleian Library (Malone 889) was bequeathed by Thomas Caldecott in 1833. It measures 5 5/16 x 3½". The frontispiece is mounted, and may possibly have come from another copy. The title-page is cropped and mutilated at the bottom. The binding is probably of the late eighteenth century. At the back of the Lucrece title-page the 'Wriothesley' dedication is copied in manuscript from the 1616 edition.

On front pastedown: "Presented to the Bodleian Library by Thos Caldecott of the Middle Temple Esq formerly fellow of New College. April 11th 1833."
