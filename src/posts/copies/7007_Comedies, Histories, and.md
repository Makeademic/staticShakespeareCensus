This issue is dated 1663, with verses "To the Reader" on A1v and the title page on [A2]r. Some copies include the portrait on the title page and some do not. Only copies with none of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1663 "verses" leaf. No title page and no additional plays present. Leaf πA3 is extended and remargined.  Leaf πA4 has the outer margin patched, shaving the catchword.  Leaves πb3,6 have, on the verso, text shaved along the outer margin. Imperfections affect text on 2G2 (patched) and 2Z6 (patched), slightly on R3 and 3F3, and very slightly on C3, V6, 2I2, 2S5, 2T5, 3K6 (patched), and 3Q5. On some pages the head rule is shaved or cropped, and on a few pages the head rule is cropped and the headline shaved.  On a few pages the side rule is shaved.  (Record of these imperfections on file.) Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
