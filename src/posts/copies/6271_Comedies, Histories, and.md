The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also all other prelims., πA1,3,4,6 and *⁴. Wants port. (on title page). Supplied in the place of the prelims. are facsims. of 1st Folio leaves: πA1; πA1+1 (title page, with the two last digits of the imprint date altered in manuscript to "32"); πA2; πA5; πA5+1; πA5+2; and πA6--bound in that order. Also wanting are 3b6, 3c6, and 3d1-4 (including the colophon). Supplied from another copy are 2B3, m2, and 3c4,5; probably supplied is 2e2. Leaves H3.4 and X2.5 are bound inside out. Imperfections affect text on 3c4,5, slightly on 2q2 and very slightly on A2,3, F1, P1, g6, q5, 2b1, 2i3, and 3b4.
