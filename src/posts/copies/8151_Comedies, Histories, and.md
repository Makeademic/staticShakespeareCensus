This issue lists H. Herringman, E. Brewster, and R. Bentley as publishers in the imprint.

From Bonhams auction catalogue: "no portrait, title laid down with loss to all but 3 letters of author's name, and 5 letters of the word "Comedies" and typographical rules, dedication leaf with loss of 15 letters of text and 2 letters of catchword and strengthened on verso, 3 other preliminary leaves with some loss to typographical rule, one with 2 tears repaired, A1-5 of text torn away with loss to typographical rule, and 8 letters of text on A2, touching 5 letters of text on A3, 6 letters of text on A4, 7 letters of catchword on A5, short marginal tears on leaves (C6, G4, O1, Bb1, and 3Y5) strengthened with pasted-on paper obscuring typographical border, small rust-hole on 2R4 and 2Z5, 2 small holes in 3D1, blank piece of margin torn away from H2 and 2F4, last leaf of text (4C2) laid down, occasional spotting and minor stains (quite heavy on 3M6 and 3N1), a few ink corrections." Clarel Rare Books catalogue adds: "The first leaf, with the frontispiece portrait on the verso over a few lines of verse, is provided in facsimile. The title-page has been laid down with loss in the upper left corner to the first five letters of 'Comedies' and all but four or five letters of the Author's name [all of which were supplied long ago in manuscript facsimile]."
