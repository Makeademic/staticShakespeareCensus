This issue lists H. Herringman, E. Brewster, and R. Bentley as publishers in the imprint.

In Folger copy 35 the "verses" leaf with port. (pi1) is trimmed to the edge of the plate and letterpress on the verso and inlaid, with the upper right corner of the port. excised. Port. in state 4. Leaf I1 (short, narrow, and wormed untypically) is supplied from another copy.  Leaf *3E1, thought to be supplied from another copy, is, although short, consistently watermarked and most likely genuine. Imperfections affect text on *3D5 verso and very slightly on T5, 2D2, 2Q3 recto, 2Z5, 3Q3, 3V2, and 4A5.
