In this issue, a new bifolium A1.2 was added, including a resetting of the verses "To the Reader" in smaller type on A1v, to make room for the engraved portrait which was moved to that page, and a title page dated 1664 on A2r. The original A1 and A2, including the title page dated 1663, were apparently intended to be removed. In some copies the original was not removed and both title pages and "To the Reader" leaves, or some combination thereof, appear at some point in the volume, as noted under particular copies. This issue also added seven additional plays, which are listed on the 1664 title page. Copies with any of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: no title leaf, no "verses" leaf, but with the additional plays. Folger copy 3 is a made-up copy comprising a base copy (mutilated at front and rear, water-stained, and damaged by fungus), many leaves from a smaller copy (inlaid), Oth. entire from another copy, and a few other leaves from other copies. Wants the title leaf (πA2) and the "verses" leaf with port. (πA1); supplied in facsim. Leaves πA3-πb6 are mutilated, affecting text, and inlaid.  Leaf πb6 is misbound, fore-edge inward. Wants 16 leaves, for some of which are supplied the leaves bearing the corresponding text in the 2nd Folio: D3.4 (supplied are 2nd Folio, D3.4, which are inlaid); H1; I1,5 (supplied are respectively 2nd Folio I1, which is remargined at the tail, and 2nd Folio I5, which is inlaid); N3.4 (supplied are 2nd Folio N3.4); 2I3.4; 3A2 (supplied is 2nd Folio y2, which is inlaid); 3I3.4; 3V3; 3Y6; 3Z1; and 4E2.3. Supplied from other copies of the 3rd Folio are 39 other leaves: D2 (inlaid); I3,6 (both inlaid); K1 (inlaid); K6 (inlaid); S1; V1 (inlaid); 2F5,6 (both inlaid); 2H5,6 (both inlaid); 2N4 (remargined at the tail); 2O2 (inlaid); 2P4; 2R3 (inlaid); 3C3.4 (both inlaid); 3D3 (remargined at the tail); 3D5 (inlaid); 3F6 (inlaid); 3H5 (inlaid); 3O6 (remargined at the tail); 3P1 (remargined at the tail); 3Q2 (inlaid); 3X1-3Z4--Oth. entire and the first page of Antony (except 3Y6 and 3Z1, which are, as per above, wanting); [par.]A2 and [par.]A5--each supplied from a different copy (but both remargined at the outer margin), and [par.]D3 (inlaid). Imperfections affect text on 2O2 (mutilated and patched; as per above, supplied from another copy), 3B3, 3L2, 3M6, 3O5 (mutilated and patched), 3Z6, and [par.]G5,6 (mutilated and patched); imperfections affect text slightly on 3B4 and very slightly on C2, S2, and X1. Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
