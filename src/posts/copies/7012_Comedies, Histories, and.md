This issue is dated 1663, with verses "To the Reader" on A1v and the title page on [A2]r. Some copies include the portrait on the title page and some do not. Only copies with none of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1663 title leaf (without the portrait), 1664 "verses" leaf, no additional plays. Both the "verses" and the title leaf are inlaid. Because this "verses" leaf is laid in, and is likely a later sophistication designed to include the portrait somewhere in the volume, and because no other distinctive aspect of the 1664 issue is present, this has been considered a copy of the 1663 issue.
