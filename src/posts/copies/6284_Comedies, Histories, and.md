The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Folger copy 54 is nominally of the first issue. However, the issue cannot be definitively determined.  This copy wants πA2 (title leaf) but contains πA5 ("Effigies" leaf) proper to the first issue (in state Ib, STC 22274a). Wants port. (on title page). Also wants πA1. Supplied in manuscript are a nonce title page with a nonce port. and a facing page bearing the text (the Ben Jonson "verses") on the page facing the t.p. of the 2nd Folio (πA1 verso). Leaves 3d3,4 are mutilated, affecting text including the colophon (only a stub of 3d4 remains), with the wanting text supplied in manuscript Imperfections affect text slightly on R5 and 3c5 and very slightly on a3, m6, 2h2, 2l5, 2t5, and 2v3.  The lower outer corner of n4-p2 is mutilated, affecting rules and catchwords passim.
