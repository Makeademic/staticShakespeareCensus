This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at the signe of the Blacke Beare" in "Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

Imperfect: Lacks preliminary leaves πA1-2, πA6, and *1; and leaves D4-E1, R2-5, Z1, c1, c5, d3, 2p6, 2z3-4, 3d2-3d4; Laid-in: Facsimile leaves replacing missing preliminary leaves and leaves D4-E1, R2-5, Z1, c1, c5, d3, 2p6, 2z3-4, 3d2-3d4. There is also evidence of leaves from other editions and second folio copies replacing original leaves; The "Effigy" leaf (πA5) is a possible facsimile. Leaf corresponds to state C, with crown watermark, as described by Todd. This state is associated with issue 1; Facsimile title page (leaf πA2) corresponds to issue 1 described by Todd, with the imprint following 1b.
