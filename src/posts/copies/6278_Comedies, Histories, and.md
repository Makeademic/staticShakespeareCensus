The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also πA1,3,4,6. Wants port. (on title page). In place of πA2 (title leaf) is supplied a leaf with, on its recto, a nonce printed title page  Onto this nonce t.p. is pasted a separate piece bearing an engraved 19th-cent. version (signed "R. C. Bell") of the Droeshout port. of Shakespeare that is proper to the Shakespeare Folios. Wants also: G2.5; 2A⁶ and 2B⁶ (most of W.T.); a3.4; 2c⁶ (end of Troilus and beginning of Cor.); 2f⁶ (part of Titus); 2h⁶ and 2i3.4 (parts of Romeo); 2k⁶ and 2l⁶ (almost all of Timon and beginning of Caesar); and 3c⁶ and 3d⁴ (most of Cymb., including the colophon). Imperfections affect text on x3 and 2i5, slightly on Z5 and 2i2, and very slightly on M3, V6, i4, n2, r5, x1, and 2e2. A short copy with many head rules and a few headlines shaved or cropped.
