In this issue, a new bifolium A1.2 was added, including a resetting of the verses "To the Reader" in smaller type on A1v, to make room for the engraved portrait which was moved to that page, and a title page dated 1664 on A2r. The original A1 and A2, including the title page dated 1663, were apparently intended to be removed. In some copies the original was not removed and both title pages and "To the Reader" leaves, or some combination thereof, appear at some point in the volume, as noted under particular copies. This issue also added seven additional plays, which are listed on the 1664 title page. Copies with any of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1664 title leaf, and the additional plays. T.p. is closely trimmed and mounted, with the first two lines touched up with pen and ink. Wants the "verses" leaf; supplied in facsim. Also wants πA3,4, πb⁶, 4B6, 4E2-4, [par.]A3.4, and [par.]G5,6. Damaged at the tail throughout, with many leaves patched or repaired with a reinforcing strip.  Text is affected by damage at tail to 23 leaves (list on file).  Many of the reinforcing strips cover text slightly or a catchword. Closely trimmed.  Text is shaved at the outer margin of 15 pages (list on file). Other imperfections affect text on 20 leaves (list on file). Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
