Heston and Williams provenance for first four tragedies only. These four were auctioned at Bonham's in 2016; A&C was purchased separately from Quaritch, except for title page which is on last verso of Othello; the duplicate title leaf from Quaritch copy sold by Cognards. A&C later bound with the other four by Kevin Oliver, who placed the Williams bookplates and the Bonham's sale tag on front pastedown. The sale tag is in an envelope, so it can be taken out and both sides can be seen; one side appears to have Heston's signature but since Heston was dead at the time of the Bonham's auction, this is likely a reproduction. Rear pastedown with Cognards' notes on provenance.
