In this issue, a new bifolium A1.2 was added, including a resetting of the verses "To the Reader" in smaller type on A1v, to make room for the engraved portrait which was moved to that page, and a title page dated 1664 on A2r. The original A1 and A2, including the title page dated 1663, were apparently intended to be removed. In some copies the original was not removed and both title pages and "To the Reader" leaves, or some combination thereof, appear at some point in the volume, as noted under particular copies. This issue also added seven additional plays, which are listed on the 1664 title page. Copies with any of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1664 "verses" leaf, and the additional plays. Wants the title leaf (πA2); supplied in facsimile. Wants the portrait proper to this edition, because the "verses" leaf (πA1) is a composite of two pieces inset--the portrait supplied from the 4th Folio (1685), the "verses" genuine (with the extremities of the first line and the last line completed in manuscript facsim.). Portrait in state 4. Wants πA3; supplied in facsim. (extended) signed "F.S by H junr. [i.e., by John Harris, Jr.]". Leaf πA4 is touched up with pen and ink. Leaves πA4-πb4 are patched. Leaves F6 and G1 (both narrow) are patched, and 3I3.4 and 3Q3.4 are remargined. Wants [par.]G5,6; supplied in facsim., each facsim. leaf signed "FS by J Harris". Imperfections affect text very slightly on Y3, 2I2, and 3K2. Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
