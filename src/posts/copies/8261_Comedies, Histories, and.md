This issue differs in the setting of the title page from the other two, and it lists H. Herringman as the publisher in the imprint and Joseph Knight and Francis Saunders as the booksellers.

In Folger copy 27, the "verses" leaf with port. (π1) and 4C2 are mounted. Imperfections affect text slightly on 3B3 and very slightly on K4,5, O1 verso, O2 verso, 2R6 verso, 2T4, 3H6, 3N6, and 4B1. K59.  Inserted is a "Drawing ... made from Droeshout’s portrait prefixed to the first folio Shakespeare and ... engraved for the Lansdowne Shakepeare [Shakespeare’s Works (London: Henry G. Bohn, 1858 and 1863)] by Mr. [John Henry] Robinson" (hinged onto the recto of the mount of π1).  With (facing) a label bearing an inscription from Henry G. Bohn presenting the drawing to A.G. Burdett-Coutts (mounted on front free endpaper verso).
