BRITISH MUSEUM. 6 15/16 x 5". Small stains on several leaves; three small holes in outer margin A2; soiled. Notes by Halliwell: "For this most rare edition (most rare in perfect state) I gave Mr. Daniel in exchange The History of Tom Thumb, 1625 bought by me at Utterson's sale for £6. 2. 6, and a volume of curious Penny Romances, no. 1516 at Utterson's sale, which cost me (with commission) £33. 10. The present volume cost me altogether therefore £39. 12. 6.," "Another perfect copy of this edition has since been discovered & sold at Sotheby's for £86." The Daniel (exchanged with), Halliwell (sold, November 9, 1858) copy. Bound in limp vellum.
