In this issue, a new bifolium A1.2 was added, including a resetting of the verses "To the Reader" in smaller type on A1v, to make room for the engraved portrait which was moved to that page, and a title page dated 1664 on A2r. The original A1 and A2, including the title page dated 1663, were apparently intended to be removed. In some copies the original was not removed and both title pages and "To the Reader" leaves, or some combination thereof, appear at some point in the volume, as noted under particular copies. This issue also added seven additional plays, which are listed on the 1664 title page. Copies with any of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1664 "verses" leaf, and the additional plays. No title leaf; supplied in facsim. (the facsim. leaf mutilated at the inner edge, with, on the recto (facsim. title page), the first word of the imprint pieced together). The "verses" page is closely trimmed and mounted, with the letterpress of the "verses" mutilated at the right edge (slightly) and at the tail, the mutilated text supplied in manuscript facsim. and touched up with pen and ink. Portrait in state 3. Wants πA3; supplied in facsim. signed "F.S by H junr. [i.e., by John Harris, Jr.]". Leaf πb6 is supplied from another copy and is misbound with the fore-edge inward. Wants [par.]A2.5. Carefully repaired throughout.  Of the prelim. leaves: πA4 is remargined at the head and outer margin and touched up with pen and ink at the head of the verso; πb1-6 are remargined at the head and outer margin (and πb6 is remargined also at the inner margin).  Many other leaves are patched.  Mutilated text is supplied in manuscript facsim. on A5 recto and verso, D1 recto, 2A1 recto and verso, and 2O4 verso. Other imperfections affect text on 3K1, slightly on 2K4, 2Z3, and [par.]E4, and very slightly on H4, S6, 2F6, 2L3, 3C4, and 4C4,6. Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
