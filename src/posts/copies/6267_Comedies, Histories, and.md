The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

For Folger copy 13, wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also all other prelims., πA1,3,4,6 and *⁴. Wants port. (on title page). Fronted by a nonce manuscript title page  Also wanting are: Temp., Two Gent., Merry Wives, M. for M., and Errors entirely (A1-I2); K3,4; u4; 2f3.4; all but the first page of Antony (2y5-3a6); and all of Cymb. (3b1-3d4, including the colophon). The Comedies present are misbound at the end of the book.  The binder has prefixed a blank bifolium to the first page of most of the plays (the exceptions being John, with which the book begins, and Hen. VIII). Imperfections affect text on a2, s3.4, and 2g2, slightly on d5, h1, and 2q6, and very slightly on 2B3, b5, c5, d3, and 2e2.  (Lower outer corners, I3-K6 and 2t6-2y2, are damaged without affecting text.)  Leaf 2y4 is backed, masking the first page of Antony on the verso.
