This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at the signe of the Blacke Beare" in "Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

In Folger copy 24, the edges of all leaves in gatherings πA and *, as well as A1-B3, are mutilated and repaired, to various extent, without affecting text except two letters on πA2 recto (title page): ES of SHAKESPEARES (completed in manuscript facsim.).  The edges of 3d1-4 are mutilated and repaired, to various extent, without affecting text except at the upper outer corner of 3d2 (completed in manuscript facsim., recto and verso). Port. (on title page) in state 3. Imperfections affect text on 2l3 (completed in manuscript), slightly on G2, N2, 2m3, and 2r4, and very slightly on I4, L1, 2A4, e1, and f6.
