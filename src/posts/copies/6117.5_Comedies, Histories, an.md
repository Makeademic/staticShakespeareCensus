This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at the signe of the Blacke Beare" in "Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

In this copy the crossbar of the "f" in "fold" in the imprint has begun to break off. Inked fingerprint in outer margin of TGV p. 32 (ZL). From auction catalogue: "Lacking pA1 “To the Reader” signed “B. I.” and signatures Bb6-Cc1 (pages 299-302). Title-page mounted; water staining at head throughout; marginal paper repairs on c3-f3. Tear on Hh4 with loss of 4 words on verso (“[And look]” and “[The Wise]”); hole on I1 with loss of a few letters; hole on u1 with loss of signature; tears without loss on I1, Aa1, f2, bb5, yy6, ddd3, and eee3; few other minor tears, some further slight occasional spotting or staining, rebacked, wear to extremities of binding." Bottom left corner of 2H6 p 124 was folded over itself during printing, resulting in a detached appearance of text and border (ZL).  Double-strike evident on inner forme of sheet tt3.tt4 in Lear. Last leaf rebacked, partially detaching.

P" (ZL). “Hotspurre” in stage direction on f1v is corrected to “Hostesse” in an early hand; “Quintus” is corrected in pencil to "Quartus” on oo4r (Mac); “Porpentine” changed to “Porcupine” on pp5r (Ham); page number corrected on xx1r from 143 to 341, and 144 to 342; "my soule" changed to "thy soule" in Ham p 187; marginal pencil "ceasing" with underline "ending" on p 288; pencil deletes duplicate "have" in "These Foyles have have all a length" and adds ? at end on p. 300. The "To be or not to be" speech in Hamlet has extensive ink corrections, including "he" to "we" in "When we have shuffled off", "The undiscovered" changed to "Th'undiscovered", "thy Horizons" to "thy orisons". Large "a" written in pencil on first page of Tempest. Pencil annotation "Road" next to "This Doll Teare-sheet should be some Rode" on 2H4 p. 81.  Pencil correction to "Actus Quintus" (erased and "Quartus" added above) in Mac.
