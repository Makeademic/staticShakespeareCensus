This issue lists H. Herringman, E. Brewster, and R. Bentley as publishers in the imprint.

Folger copy 32 is interleaved (except for the prelims. and additional plays) with blank paper (every page ruled at the center vertically, delineating two columns), extending the book to two volumes, the volumes dividing between 2O6 (end of 2 Hen. VI) and 2P1 (beginning of 3 Hen. VI). Wants "verses" leaf with port. ([pi]1). Title leaf ([pi]2) is mutilated at the tail, affecting the imprint on the title page, and is cut down to the rules on the t.p. and mounted. Leaf [superscript pi]A1 is mounted. Wants C3.4 and Q6. Imperfections affect text slightly on *3D4 and very slightly on A5, B3, E2, K1, L1, 2G1, 2P5,*3D6, and 4B4.
