This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at his shop at the signe of the Blacke Beare in Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

In Folger copy 14, πA1 is wanting (supplied in facsim.), and the other leaves of the prelim. gathering and *1.4 are mounted, or remargined in whole or in part, or patched.  Mutilations affect two letters on πA2 recto (title page): SH of SHAKESPEARES.  Leaves πA5,6 are misbound after πA2.  Leaves *2.3 are misbound after πA3. Port. (on title page) in state 3. Leaf 2e2 is wanting; in place is a facsimile of Fo. 1, 2b5 verso and 2b6 recto (printed respectively on the recto and verso of one leaf).  Leaf 2e5 is wanting; in place is a facsim. of Fo. 1, 2c2 verso and 2c3 recto (printed respectively on the recto and verso of one leaf).  Leaves 2v3.4 are wanting; in place are facsims. of Fo. 1, 2s5,6.  Leaf 3d4 is wanting (including the colophon); supplied in manuscript facsim. Imperfections affect text on e5, slightly on O3, V5, a1, 6, 2a4, 2k5, 2n1, and 3a3.4, and very slightly on A1, K3, V6.
