The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also πA1,3,4,6. Wants port. (on title page). Wants *1. Wants 3b1-end (all of Cymb., including the colophon). Wants 2f2-5 (part of Titus). The upper outer corner of X5,6 and of Y1 is mutilated, affecting text on X5,6.  Leaf X6 is patched, with the missing text supplied in manuscript (early 18th-cent.). The lower outer corner of a4 is mutilated, affecting text. The lower outer corner of 2z6 and of 3a1-6 is mutilated.  The corner of 2z6 and of 3a3 is patched, with the missing text supplied in manuscript (early 18th-cent.).  Traces of manuscript on 3a1,4,5 indicate that the corner of those leaves was once patched, with the missing text supplied in manuscript  Leaf 3a6 was probably once patched, with the missing text supplied in manuscript  Leaf 3a5 is mutilated at the head, affecting text, and 3a6 at the upper outer corner, affecting text. Other imperfections affect text slightly on 2d2 and very slightly on A5, B5, Z3, 2B3, a3, 2b3, 2h3, and 2t4. Some head rules and headlines are shaved or cropped; list on file.
