This edition exists in three distinct states: the first includes copies that were sold without Troilus and Cressida; the second contains Troilus and Cressida but without its prologue and with a redundant final page of Romeo and Juliet crossed out by the printer; the third includes a cancel leaf with the Prologue of Troilus on its recto and the first page of the play, reprinted from a new setting of type, on its verso. In none of the states is Troilus included in the "Catalogue" of plays.

This volume contains a number of manuscript notations including the names John Kennedy, John Hammond, Jonathan Sly George Savoy, Robert Clarke, Walter Rawleigh, and Rawlinson Mayor. It is difficult to tell what these names refer to since the context within which they are found does not give us any clues .... The missing leaves remove the following plays from the volume: The Tempest, The Two Gentlemen of Verona, The Merry Wives of Windsor, Measure for Measure, Richard III, Othello (except the first page), Antony and Cleopatra, and Cymbeline.
