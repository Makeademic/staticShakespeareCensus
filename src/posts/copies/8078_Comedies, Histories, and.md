This issue lists H. Herringman, E. Brewster, and R. Bentley as publishers in the imprint.

Split front inner joint. Paper repairs using Japanese tissue to front endleaf. Trimmed textblock decorated with red speckling at head, tail and foredge. Small rust marks and holes infrequently throughout. Small tear to The Taming of the Screw, page 203 located in lower right corner. Area of discolouration located at centre of page The Winter’s Tale, page 255. Small tear to tail of The first Part of King Henry the Fourth. Loss to upper corner of The second Part of King Henry the Fourth, page 73/74. Printing error to left column of The second Part of King Henry the Fourth, page 76. Small tear to tail of The second Part of King Henry the Fourth, page 85/86.  Tear and crease to tail of folio The second Part of King Henry the Sixth, page 141/2. Small tear to tail of folio of The third Part of King Henry the Sixth, page 159/160. Small tear to tail of The Life and Death of Richard the Third, page 191/2.  Loss to upper corner of The Tragedy of Macbeth, page 41/42. Paper manufacturing error, hole to The Tragedy of Hamlet, page 77/78. Misprint to The Life and Death of the Lord Cromwell, page 232. Tear to foredge at the tail of A Yorkshire Tragedy, page 283/4.

Front endleaf, verso has annotation in carbon ink “Milener Millener” located near the head. The Merchant of Venice, page 152 annotations include underlining and note at tail of folio “Prima nocte domum claude, negui in vias pub cantu querula doprice libia or: l.3.0.7" [Horace's Odes, 3.7]. The first Part of King Henry the Fourth, page 45, annotation with iron gall ink. The Life and Death of Richard the Third, page 186, annotated with iron gall ink referencing Homer. Annotation in iron gall ink near head of The Tragedy of Hamlet page 70 with ink offset onto the recto of The Tragedy of Hanlet, page 71. Pencil annotation to first flyleaf at the back of the volume. Library label attached to pastedown at back of volume.
