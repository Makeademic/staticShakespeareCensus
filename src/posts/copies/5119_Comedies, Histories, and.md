In none of the states is Troilus included in the "Catalogue" of plays.

Augustin Daly (1838-1899), one of the owners of this copy, was a major American theatre owner and director. He built and owned the Daly's Theatres of New York and London .... Daly directed numerous productions of Shakespeare which were the focus of intense criticism by George Bernard Shaw.
