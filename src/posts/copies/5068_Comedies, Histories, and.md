In none of the states is Troilus included in the "Catalogue" of plays.

One owner of this copy was Sir John Evans, K.C.B., F.R.S. (1823-1908). Evans was originally a paper manufacturer by trade, but made his mark in the world as an archaeologist and numismatist. In particular, Evans’ study of fossils of the archaeopteryx demonstrated a connection between the prehistoric animal and modern birds, lending credence to Charles Darwin’s recently published theory regarding the origin of species.
