A narrow copy, with the outer rule of many text pages shaved or cropped and on a few pages the catchword or text shaved or cropped. Imperfections affect text on A4, 2G1, *3E2, and 3N1 and very slightly on H6, P2, R2, 2S1, *3E4, 3B6, 3P3, 3R6, and 4B3.
