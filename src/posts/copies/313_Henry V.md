BIRMINGHAM. 7 x 5 7/16". With manuscript notes by J. O. Halliwell. The George Smith (July, 1867, No. 2578), Halliwell (presented, April, 1867) copy. Bound in red levant morocco, by Bedford.

MS note bound in front lists edition as 1608: "(Fifteen years earlier than the first folio) Presented by J.O, Halliwell Esq. to the former Shakespeare Mem'l Library. Saved from the Fire (1879) and added to the present Library. A copy was bought by Bain at the Perkins sale July 1889 for £99" -- and on reverse: "Rosenbach Company's catalogue of Shakespeare Quartos, 1920, No. 7. $2250." \r\n\r\nOn front flyleaf: "This copy has the original last leaf (blank) of sig. G. If I recollect rightly, a copy bought by Lilly at Sotheby's, 21 March, 1864, for £12, also had this leaf, but it is rarely met with. The present very fine copy was purchased at the sale of the library of the late George Smith Esq. in 1867, lot 2578, for £10. 10. 0. Presented to the Birmingham Shakespeare Library, 6 April, 1867, by J.O. Halliwell."  Booksellers' codes on rear flyleaf.
