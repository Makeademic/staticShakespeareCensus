This issue differs in the setting of the title page from the other two, and it lists H. Herringman as the publisher in the imprint and Joseph Knight and Francis Saunders as the booksellers.

In Folger copy 9, the "verses" leaf with port. (π1) and the title leaf (π2) are mounted (margins excised). Leaf πA4 is misbound immediately following its conjugate, πA1, and before πA2.3. Leaves 2Z1.6 (short) are perhaps supplied from another copy. Imperfections affect text on 2D6 and 3P1, slightly on 2X4, and very slightly on A1, G3, R3, 2S1, 2T5, *3E2, and 3F4.
