The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Folger copy 58 is nominally of the first issue, as formulated by William B. Todd, "Issues and states of the Second Folio and Milton’s epitaph on Shakespeare," Studies in bibliography, v. 5 (1952-53), p. 81-108. However, the issue cannot be definitively determined. This copy wants πA2 (title leaf) but contains πA5 ("Effigies" leaf) proper to the first issue (in state Ib). Wants port. (on title page). Wants s4 and 3d3,4 (including colophon). Leaves c3,4, i6, and 3c2 are mutilated and patched, affecting text; text on c3,4 and i6 wanting or covered by patch supplied in manuscript. Other imperfections affect text slightly on t1 and very slightly on 15 other leaves (list on file).
