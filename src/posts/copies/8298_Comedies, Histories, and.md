The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1685 folio edition.

Folger copy 18 wants the "verses" leaf with port., [pi]1, and the title leaf, [pi]2 (inserted is a nonce title page dated 1632, with a facsim. port.). Also wants all else before G1 (other prelims., Temp., Two Gent., and Merry Wives, and all but last leaf of M. for M.). Remainder of the comedies, G1-Z4, misbound at end. Also wants 2T3.4 and 3T6-end (all the additional plays except Pericles and London prodigal). Leaves G1-05 are tattered and torn with a burned spot in some leaves, affecting text on G1-I5 and L5, text very slightly on M6, N1, and O3, and many page numbers G1-O4. Other imperfections affect text on Y3, Z3, 2B4, 2E4, 2F5, 2G1, 2I4, 2S1, 2T5, *3D3,4, and 3A4, slightly on 2U1, and very slightly on 16 other leaves (list on file). Leaf Z4 is mounted.
