ELIZABETHAN CLUB. 7 1/16 x 5¼". G4 genuine but partly lacking. Stabbed\r\nthroughout on inner margins. "15. 7. 6. ELIZABETHAN CLUB. 7 1/16 x 5¼". Stabbed throughout on inner margins.\r\n"15. 7. 6. Sale by Sotheby, 1821" in Heber's hand and Heber's stamp on front\r\nfly-leaf; G1 genuine but partly lacking; Huth book-plate. The Heber (June,\r\n1834, No. 5461), Daniel (July, 1864, No. 1431), Huth (November, 1911, No.\r\n1208), Cochran (presented, December, 1911) copy. Bound in vellum.

Manuscript note by Richard Heber on front endpaper, and Bibliotheca Heberaina stamp; sold as lot 5461 in the Heber sale by Sotheby's, 28 June 1834. Sold as lot 1431 in the George Daniel sale by Sotheby's, 26 July 1864. The Huth copy (with bookplate) acquired by Mr. Cochran in November 1911 prior to the public sale. Gift of Alexander S. Cochran, December 1911.
