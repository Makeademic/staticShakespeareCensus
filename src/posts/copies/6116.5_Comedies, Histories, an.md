In this copy the crossbar of the "f" in "fold" in the imprint has begun to break off. Capital italic A once accidentally set with typeface facing down on the forme on p. 142 of JC, the compositors having fixed the other instance as shown in SC 6184.5, e.g. (ZL). Silk bookmark in gutter of opening pp. 82-83 in H5. From auction catalogue: "Title and final leaf trimmed and inlaid, title with imprint just shaved, first six leaves re-hinged; occasional soiling and spotting, A1 with soiling ... some leaves shaved closed to rule, rust-hole on S3 touching one letter."
