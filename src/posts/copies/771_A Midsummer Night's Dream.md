529

ELIZABETHAN CLUB. 7 5/8 x 5¼". Wormhole in lower blank margin of\r\nsome leaves. Bibliotheca Heberiana stamp on fly-leaf: "Bindley's sale, Pt.\r\nIII, 2040, Feb. 1819" in Heber's hand, inside front cover; Huth book-plate. The\r\nBindley (February, 1819, No. 2040), Heber (June, 1834, No. 5442), Daniel (July,\r\n1864, No. 1434), Huth (November, 1911, No. 1212), Cochran (presented, December,\r\n1911) copy. Bound in paper covers, uncut.

Bibliotheca Heberiana stamp on endpaper, lot 5442 in the Heber sale, Sotheby's, 28 June 1834; on the endpaper is a note, probably by Heber, stating that this copy had come from "Bindleys Sale Pt. III 2040--Feb. 1819." This copy was also owned by George Daniel, and it was sold with his books, by Sotheby's, on 26 July 1864, lot 1425, purchased by Lilly. The Huth copy (with bookplate) acquired by Mr. Cochran in November 1911 prior to the public sale. Gift of Alexander S. Cochran, December 1911.
