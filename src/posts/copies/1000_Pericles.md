A perfect copy of thirty-six leaves, belonging to Mr Marsden J. Perry, measures 6 7/8 x 5 3/8". It is unbound, and with it is stitched up Samuel Daniel's The Queen's Arcadia (1606). On the title-page are the autographs of two former owners, 'Edw. Palmer' and 'Jno. Fenn', 1782. The latter was Sir John Fenn (1739-94), editor of the 'Paston Letters', who owned the 1624 edition of Lucrece (Census No. XXII). The copy was bought for the present owner at the sale of John Chaloner Smith's library, on February 12, 1896, for £171.
