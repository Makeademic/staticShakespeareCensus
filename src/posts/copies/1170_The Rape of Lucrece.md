The Rowfant copy, which formerly belonged to Frederick Locker Lampson, has the inscription on title-page: 'Pretium 4 N : L : S :' It measures 5½ x 3 7/16". It at one time belonged to Narcissus Luttrell (1657-1732), and seems to have been sold at the Ouvry sale in 1882, for £31, to Messrs. Ellis \r\nand White, the booksellers of Bond Street. It was acquired by Messrs. Dodd, Mead & Co., booksellers of New York, in 1904.

Narcissus Luttrell, monogram N:L:S: and price inscription: "pretium 4d" (title page); W.C. Van Antwerp, his sale, Sotheby’s, 22 March 1907, lot 198 to Quartich (with collation note by F.S. Ferguson on rear pastedown) for Morgan; Pierpont Morgan, purchased with the Van Antwerp collection, 1907.
