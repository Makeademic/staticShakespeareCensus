The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also all other prelims., πA1,3,4,6 and *⁴. Wants port. (on title page). Also wants: part of Temp. (A1-6); E1; Q1,2; all of AYL and part of Shrew (Q3-T1, T6); Z1; i3.4; u1; most of Hen. VIII (u5-x4, x6, and y1,6); and 3d3,4 (including the colophon). Rich. III (s1-u4) and then John (a1-b5) are misbound as the last two Histories.  Leaves I1-5 are loose and protruding from the textblock and are accordingly damaged and discolored at the fore-edge and tail. Mutilated are: d6 and l4, affecting text; 2y2,3 and 3b3, affecting catchwords and rules; and 3d1,2, affecting text very slightly and catchwords and rules.  From 2v4 to the end, worming touches text on many leaves and affects readings slightly on 2z3 and very slightly on 3a2, 3b1,2, and 3c1,2,5.  Other imperfections affect text slightly on m4 and very slightly on D4, H3, 2B5, o5, s4, and 2v1.
