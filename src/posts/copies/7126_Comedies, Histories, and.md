In this issue, a new bifolium A1.2 was added, including a resetting of the verses "To the Reader" in smaller type on A1v, to make room for the engraved portrait which was moved to that page, and a title page dated 1664 on A2r. The original A1 and A2, including the title page dated 1663, were apparently intended to be removed. In some copies the original was not removed and both title pages and "To the Reader" leaves, or some combination thereof, appear at some point in the volume, as noted under particular copies. This issue also added seven additional plays, which are listed on the 1664 title page. Copies with any of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1664 title leaf and additional plays. From auction catalogue: "Lacking the first leaf with portrait and verses 'To the reader,' title cut down and mounted without loss of text, some creasing of earlier leaves, occasional small soil marks and isolated brown spots, 3F3 and 3M5 holed with loss to several lines recto and verso, smaller rust holes affecting a few letters, T1 torn at upper outer corner with some loss to rule and partial loss of page numeral, R1 with clean tear into text, 3F6 with repaired paper fault causing slight loss, other paper faults to 2F5 and 2I1, 4C1 with crease affecting alignment of several lines, 4E4 with old paper repair on blank verso, staining of text on N3 and R4, P5-Q2 with worm track at margin, I2 and Z2 torn and 4C3-4 frayed at margin, some marginal browning and occasional soiling, final leaf soiled on verso."
