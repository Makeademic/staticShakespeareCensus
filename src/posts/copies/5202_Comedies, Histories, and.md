This edition exists in three distinct states: the first includes copies that were sold without Troilus and Cressida; the second contains Troilus and Cressida but without its prologue and with a redundant final page of Romeo and Juliet crossed out by the printer; the third includes a cancel leaf with the Prologue of Troilus on its recto and the first page of the play, reprinted from a new setting of type, on its verso. In none of the states is Troilus included in the "Catalogue" of plays.

All preliminaries are facsimiles. πA1+1 has a facsimile of a state two portrait; it is remargined. πA3 is attached to a new inner margin; there is MS just below the text near the inner corner: 'FS by Harris'. (Rasmussen and West)

The binding is dark red leather (not morocco) with moderate texturing. There is a double gold-tooled border on the front and back boards. The outer border is composed of a triple gold fillet with a triangle border on the outer edge. The inner border contains a vine, flower, and thistle flower pattern on the inside, then a triple gold fillet, then a fleur-de-lis border on the outer edge with a flower and thistle flower decoration at each outer corner.
