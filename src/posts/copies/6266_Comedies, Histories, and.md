The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

For Folger copy 12, wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also all other prelims., πA1,3,4,6 and *⁴. Wants port. (on title page). Wants 3d⁴ (including the colophon).  A leaf is inserted at end that bears in manuscript (18th-cent.) the text proper to 3d4 recto, on the recto, and "The Tragedy of Cymbeline.  Dramatis Personae", on the verso. Wants: A1 (supplied is 1st Folio leaf A1, bearing the corresponding text); D1; and M6. Supplied from another copy are: d6 (inserted loose; a portion at the tail of the each column of text has been neatly excised); k5; o6; 2e4; 2e5 (probably supplied); 2f1; and 2h3 (likely supplied). A short and narrow copy; many marginal rules are shaved or cropped, and text is shaved passim. Other imperfections affect text on D2, slightly on E1 and n5, and very slightly on E4, 2d2, and 2n1.
