This issue lists Robert Allot as the publisher in the imprint and lists it as "to be fold at his shop at the signe of the Blacke Beare in Pauls Church-yard" [DEEP]

Folger copy 16 is a carefully repaired copy, with all of the preliminary leaves and A1 mounted and/or patched and completed in manuscript facsim. and/or touched up with pen and ink; πA1 is also closely trimmed. Port. (on title page) in state 3. Parts supplied from other copies are: Y, which is remargined; probably c1, as suggested by tear (repaired); c2-6, as evidenced by congruent tears (repaired); k and l1-4, as evidenced by remargining and the redundancy of watermarks on l1 and l6; u1-5, which are remargined; y1-2, which are remargined similarly to each other and as evidenced by the redundancy of watermarks on y1 and y6 and on y2 and y5; and y3-5, which are remargined similarly to each other. Imperfections affect text on d2 and 2p1, slightly on A3, E2, 2f2, and 2h2, and very slightly on C6. Leaves 3a2-3d3 are consistently wormed, except 3b1, which (inlaid) is supplied from another copy.  The cited worming slightly or very slightly affects text passim.  Leaves 3c6-3d3 are remargined or only extended or extended and patched. Leaf 3d4 is wanting; supplied in photo-facsim.
