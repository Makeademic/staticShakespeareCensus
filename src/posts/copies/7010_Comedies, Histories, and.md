This issue is dated 1663, with verses "To the Reader" on A1v and the title page on [A2]r. Some copies include the portrait on the title page and some do not. Only copies with none of the distinctive features of the 1664 issue are listed here.

Includes: 1663 "verses" leaf, 1663 title leaf, no additional plays. Title page is cut down and inlaid, includes the portrait in state 3. The "Verses" leaf is cut down and inlaid. Leaf πA4 is misbound before πA3. Imperfections affect text slightly on L2, 2O1, 3B6, and 3R2 and very slightly on K1, 2O4, 3F4, and 3V4. Variant states and printings, as recorded by James G. McManaway, "New discoveries in the Third Folio," PBSA, v. 70, (1976), 469-80, and other such variations, are identified in file.
