The copies listed here cannot be identified with any one specific issue of the 1632 folio edition.

Wants both πA2 (title leaf) and πA5 ("Effigies" leaf). Wants also all other prelims., πA1,3,4,6, and *⁴. Wants port. (on title page). Also wants A1 and all of Cymb. (3b1-3d4, including the colophon). Leaves V3.4 are misbound inside out.  Leaves y3.4 and 2g3.4 are short and damaged at the head and fore-edge but are integral to this copy (damaged while once loose and protruding). With small holes in many leaves.  Imperfections affect text on s4 and 2y3, slightly on 10 others, and very slightly on 29 others; list on file.  This copy is short, with many rules (especially at the head) and a few headlines shaved or cropped.
